About Slice Heaven

Our story and commitment to quality

Founded in 2025, Slice Heaven has been serving artisanal pizzas made with locally-sourced ingredients and traditional techniques. Our master pizzaiolos bring decades of experience from Naples, Italy, the birthplace of pizza.

We're committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging and supporting local farmers. Every pizza is hand-tossed and baked in our wood-fired ovens for that perfect crisp.
